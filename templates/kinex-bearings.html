RAILWAY INDUSTRY

KINEX BEARINGS offers comprehensive services in the field of research, development and production of rolling bearings. One of the most important industrial segments in terms of sales is the railway industry.

We date the beginning of production in the segment of rolling bearings for rolling stock to 1959 .

Currently, KINEX BEARINGS is one of the leading suppliers of bearings for railway vehicle axles on the European market and has authorizations for the supply of various products for use in the railway industry and in other countries. The production of single-row ball bearings used in the railway industry is ensured in accordance with the requirements of European standards EN 12080 .

KINEX BEARINGS, as also offers deliveries of bearing housings for freight wagons with a load of 22.5 tons and 25 tons per axle.

PRODUCTS:

> Single row ball bearings
> Single-row roller bearings
> Bearing boxes for freight wagons

INDUSTRIAL DIVISION
